About

Aditya College of Engineering, Madanapalle

Explore More

Aditya College of Engineering, Madanapalle under the umbrella of Veda Educational Society was Established in the year 2009 on lofty and noble ideals to impart excellent technical and value based Education under the able and dynamic leadership of Sri R. Ramamohan Reddy, Secretary & Correspondent and Sri .M. Nagamalla Reddy, President who are young ,dynamic and committed to provide the best education to the students.

ACEM is beautifully nestled against an array of mountains and lush greenery about 10 km from the heart of Madanapalle. In response to the needs of students in today’s rapidly changing world, we equip our students with effective learning techniques to succeed and to obtain a bright future in safety environment. The college has a wide reputation for providing outstanding teaching and learning and has many first class facilities.
